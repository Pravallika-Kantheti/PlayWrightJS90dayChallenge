## General

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|

## Tips

- **Customize keybindings:** Press `Ctrl + K Ctrl + S` to open Keyboard Shortcuts and search or edit any shortcut.
- **Keybinding JSON:** You can also edit `keybindings.json` directly for advanced customizations.
- **Chord keybindings:** Some shortcuts require pressing multiple keys in sequence (e.g., `Ctrl + K`, then `Ctrl + C` for adding a comment).
